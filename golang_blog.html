Golang intro

Jan, 2023

Before we start deep diving into using variables in Go-lang, we should first understand the fundamental need for why we use variables in the first place.

Lets say you are creating a social media network for all the IPL lovers to share your favorite moments from the Indian Premier League. You will sign up users on that platform and each one of them will have their name, age, favorite team, favorite player, etc. All of these properties are the data that you will be storing. You can think of computer memory as a sequence of boxes that will hold your data.

The properties like name, favorite team, and the favorite player can be stored as text(strings) while age would be a number. This is important to understand because Go is what is called a statically typed language. This means that you first have to declare the data types of the data you want to store into variables.
